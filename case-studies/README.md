[中文说明](#中文说明) | [English overview](#english-overview) | [Document index](#document-index)

## English overview

This library brings together YouTricot case studies, sourcing guides, technical white papers, and production playbooks for fashion brands, wholesalers, product developers, and knitwear sourcing teams. The collection covers low-MOQ production, sampling, yarn selection, factory evaluation, quality control, EU compliance, costing, and China-based OEM/ODM execution.

Use these documents to prepare supplier discussions, improve technical briefs, compare sourcing models, and identify the evidence that should be confirmed before sampling or bulk production. Commercial figures, lead times, certifications, and compliance claims are project-specific and should be reconfirmed for each order and destination market.

## 中文说明

本资料库汇集 YouTricot 的针织服装案例研究、采购指南、技术白皮书和生产操作框架，适合服装品牌、批发商、产品开发人员及采购团队参考。内容覆盖低起订量生产、打样、纱线选择、工厂评估、质量控制、欧盟合规、成本拆解及中国针织 OEM/ODM 项目执行。

这些资料可用于供应商沟通、技术包准备、采购模式比较，以及在打样或大货生产前建立验证清单。文中的价格、交期、认证和合规信息可能因产品、订单和目标市场而变化，实际项目中应再次确认。
